My Projects

Optimal Segmentation For Thermal Hotspot In Liquid Body Using Deep Neural Network

Developed a custom dataset using a thermal camera to identify thermal hotspots in liquids. Currently, working on an unsupervised segmentation framework to autonomously separate and label distinct regions.

Brain Tumor Image Segmentation Using Federated Learning

Developing a federated learning approach for the segmentation of brain tumor images across diverse datasets, aiming to refine the precision of medical image segmentation through the implementation of a own made loss function. The goal is to advance the diagnosis and treatment of brain tumors.

Night Vision Face Detection

Implemented night vision face detection using YOLOv8, HOG, SVM, and CNN models, showcasing proficiency in cutting-edge computer vision algorithms for accurate low-light face detection.
